Description

Infected Dawn is a 3D point-and-click style adventure game. You play as a survivor in the zombie apocalypse, with a horde of undead moving towards your hideout. They will arrive at sundown, so you need to explore the surrounding areas to find whatever you can to survive.

How to Play

  • W, A, S, D - Move
  • Mouse - Look
  • Space - Jump
  • Shift - Run
  • Left Click - Interact / Grab & Throw Object
  • Right Click - Drop Object
  • M - Open Map
  • Tab / I - Open Inventory

Walkthrough

View Walkthrough
  • Enter the front door of the house and grab the Water Bottle from the counter.
  • Pick up the Radio (without batteries) from the dining table and then head into the library to grab the Box of Matches.
  • Still in the library, open the top drawer and grab the Batteries.
  • Exit the house and go around back to grab the Jumper Cables on top of the car.
  • Travel to the Diner and retrieve the Wrench from the supply closet behind the kitchen.
  • Travel to the Bus Stop, go around the side of the bus to grab the Gas Can, then move to the front of the bus and use the Wrench to remove the Car Battery.
  • Travel to the Golf Course and install the Car Battery into the dead Golf Cart to reduce travel time.
  • With the golf cart, drive to the center of the course and pick up the Lighter Fluid near the hole.
  • Head to the Trailer Park and collect the Bolt Cutters on the second house on the right. Also, pick up the Shovel leaning against the first trailer home on the left.
  • Go to the first trailer home on the right and connect the Generator to the fence using the Jumper Cables.
  • Refuel and start the Generator with the Gas Can.
  • Use the Lighter Fluid on the garbage pile and ignite it using the Matches.
  • Visit the Laundromat, collect the Cleaning Chemicals, and fill the Water Bottle up with the water cooler behind the counter.
  • Combine the Cleaning Chemicals with the Water Bottle to create a mixture.
  • Return to the Golf Course and use the Bolt Cutters to unlock the shed. Pour the chemical mixture into the irrigation system and collect the Screwdriver.
  • Craft the Batteries and Radio together.
  • Locate the PA system outside the Clubhouse and connect the Radio with batteries to it.
  • Return home and use the Shovel to dig a hole next to the path.
  • Finally, use the Screwdriver to fix the front door.
