Infected Dawn

About

Infected Dawn is an atmospheric 3D point-and-click game about time management and preparation for an undead infection.

Walkthrough

View Walkthrough
  • Enter the front door of the house and grab the Water Bottle from the counter.
  • Pick up the Radio from the dining table and then head into the library to grab the Box of Matches.
  • Exit the house and go around back to grab the Jumper Cables.
  • Travel to the Diner and retrieve the Wrench from the supply closet behind the kitchen.
  • Travel to the Bus Stop, go around the side of the bus to grab the Gas Can, then move to the front of the bus and use the Wrench to remove the Car Battery.
  • Travel to the Golf Course and install the Car Battery into the dead Golf Cart to reduce travel time.
  • Head to the Trailer Park and collect the Bolt Cutters on the far back left house. Also, pick up the Shovel leaning against the middle right trailer home.
  • Go behind the far-right trailer home and connect the Generator to the fence using the Jumper Cables.
  • Refuel and start the Generator with the Gas Can.
  • Go to the large garbage pile in the trench and ignite it using the Matches.
  • Visit the Laundromat and collect the Cleaning Chemicals.
  • Combine the Cleaning Chemicals with the Water Bottle to create a mixture.
  • Return to the Golf Course and use the Bolt Cutters to unlock the shed. Pour the chemical mixture into the irrigation system and collect the Screwdriver.
  • Locate the PA system outside the Clubhouse and connect your Radio to it.
  • Return home and use the Shovel to dig a hole next to the path.
  • Finally, use the Screwdriver to fix the front door.
