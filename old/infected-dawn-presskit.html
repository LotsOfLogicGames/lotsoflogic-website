Description

Infected Dawn is a 3D point-and-click adventure game in a pixel art style. You play as a survivor in the zombie apocalypse, with a horde of undead moving towards your hideout. They will arrive at sundown, so you need to explore the surrounding areas to find whatever you can to survive.
